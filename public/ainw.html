Alice in Wonderland

Alice in Wonderland is an XR performance piece made at the LSU Virtual Production Studio. The piece is retelling of the growing and shrinking scenes from Alice in Wonderland. A live actor interacts with the LED screen, creating an interactive story. My contribution was the concept, the programming, the music, and the sound design.

The project was made in Unreal Engine 5.0.

Team:

Programming Lead/Sound Design/Music: Treya Nash
Door Room/Wonderland Design: Samira Awad
