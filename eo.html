Electronic Opera Research

For the past 2 years I have been doing research on electronic opera, focussing on opera in the 1950s-2000s. In 2022, I presented my research on Alice Shields opera Apocalypse at Rethinking the History of Technology-based Music, a coference organised by the Centre for Research in New Music at the University of Huddersfield. Look out for a paper on the broader history of electronic opera coming soon!
