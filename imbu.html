I Might Be Underwater

Instrumentation: flute and live electronics

Year: 2020 | Duration: ~6'30"

Program Note: I Might Be Underwater was originally written to be performed over zoom during the pandemic. The flutist would connect to a max patch on another computer using soundflower, and would trigger the patch using pitch detection. The version in the video was recorded live. The title of the piece comes from Miriam Nash's poem "Sink," found in her 2017 collection, All the Prayers in the House the title phrase appears throughout the piece in various forms as the flutist guides the patch through improvisation.
